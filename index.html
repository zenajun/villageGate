Directions and Hours

We are located at 1357 Bathurst street in Toronto, on the north-east corner of Bathurst and Nina. Across the road to the south is Hillcrest Public School.

Customer parking available!

We're open 7 days a week 8:00 am - 7:00 pm

We're also open on holidays!

Meet our team

At Village Gate Animal Hospital our Mission is to provide professional, compassionate care for dogs and cats in a way that is convenient for You.

Above all do no harm
Hippocrates

Dr. Geoff Toole D.V.M.

Dr.Geoff Toole graduated from the Ontario Veterinary College in 1997. He began his career working evening and overnight shifts in a busy 24 hour clinic in Toronto. After 2 years he was offered a position as manager of a clinic in the Beaches where he practiced for 3 years. Dr. Toole also ran a veterinary house call service. In 2003 Geoff established the Village Gate Animal Hospital in central Toronto.
